Women Safety

1. Be positive, confident and stay alert.
2. Choose safe/well recognised place/mall for shopping. Avoid lonely places.
3. Keep distance with unknown people. Don’t try to make them friends. They can be criminals or kidnappers.
4. Don't go to unknown places or stay there during night.
5. Always select the reputed beauty parlor. Make sure that it is safe and secure with your privacy.
6. Always carry your mobile phone, so that you can call police or family member when you are in trouble.
7. Do not share plans for your journey with your friends.
8. Choose a safe vehicle for journey.
9. Don't open your identity on phone when your are travlling.
10. Select a reputed hotel for staying. Don't go for hotels at deserted area.
11. Investigate the room before use.
12. Always close the door. Use safety latch also.
13. Use peephole before open the door. Don’t allow to enter unknown person.
14. Keep an eye on your surroundings.
