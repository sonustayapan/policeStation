Mobile Safety

1. Keep your mobile phone device with you. Do not leave it anywhere.
2. Read the manufacturer's instructions carefully and follow "HOW TO SET" instructions for your phone.
3. Write the 15 digit IMEI no. at a diary. In case your mobile phone is lost, this IMEI no will be asked at police station and will be needed to track the phone.
4. Activate automatic keypad locking system on your mobile which can be unlocked using password. This way unnecessary key pressings can be avoided.
6. If you have lost your mobile phone, lodge complaint at your nearest police station.
7. Use mobile tracking feature to find your lost mobile set. You need to set 2 mobile no. also. In this feature - whenever someone inserts the new SIM card to your lost mobile set, this feature will send SMS to those mobile nos.
8. Close the applications (camera, audio/video player) and connections (bluetooth, infrared, wi-fi) when you don't need them.
9. When you sell your mobile set to somebody, ensure to erase all your personal records.
10. Keep regular back-up of your mobile set.
