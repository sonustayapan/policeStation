Senior Citizen Safety

1. Generally, senior citizens face problem with the known people like servant, guard, driver etc. So be alert with them.
2. Before keeping a helping-hand at home try to get information about him like name, permanent address etc. Also verify the same with your local police station.
3. Never discuss about your property, bank saving account in front of servant or unknown person.
4. Do not leave your servant alone at home.
5. Do not allow servant’s relatives or friend at your home.
6. Behave gently with your driver, guard and servant.
7. Keep your valuable items at bank vaults/lockers.
8. Install latest safety instruments like door alarm, electronic eye bell, etc. at home..
9. Use peephole at your main entrance. Identify the person before allowing him or her in your house.
10. Keep your keys in a safe place. Don’t keep it in open place.
11. When you call somebody for maintenance work, first check his identify.
12. Use phone book to remember phone nos.
13. Inform your neighbours about your guests.
